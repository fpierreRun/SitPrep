Step 1/2: In case your home becomes inaccessible, where is your designated emergency meeting place?

Factors to consider

  • Choose an accessible and familiar location, like a landmark, intersection, or public park, known to everyone in the family.

  • If you have a pet, ensure the location is animal-friendly.

  • Consider a location with a nearby water source for emergency hydration and sanitation needs.

  • Consider having multiple meeting locations as backup options.

Your privacy and safety is important to us. SitPrep and any other entity do not save, store, or share the information you provide.

Meeting Place

It is a good idea to choose an emergency meeting place for your household in case you are apart during an emergency. Be sure to select a place that is accessible and known to everyone, such as a landmark, intersections, or public park. If you have a pet, make sure the location is animal friendly.

Step 2/2: What is your evacuation route to the nearest shelter in case of an emergency?

Factors to consider

  • Plan how and where to evacuate with your household.

  • Identify multiple emergency destinations (friend's house,community center, motel, etc) in different directions.

  • Arrange for pet accommodation as most shelters allow only service animals.

  • Explore alternative evacuation routes and transportation options.

  • Follow local officials' guidance and be prepared to walk if needed.
