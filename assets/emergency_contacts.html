Step 1/1: In case of an emergency, who will be the designated contacts for your household?

Factors to consider

Consider the folowing factors when choosing designated contacts:

  • Proximity: Keep in mind it might be easier to make a long distance call than to call nearby during a disaster.

  • Reliability & Responsiveness: Dependable individuals who respond promptly to emergency communications.

  • Familiarity with Plans: Contacts who know your emergency plans, routes, and meeting points.

  • Multiple Contacts: Have several contacts to increase successful communication chances.

  • Mutual Agreement: Obtain consent from designated contacts for their role in the emergency plan.

  • Designated Roles: Assign specific responsibilities to each contact for better coordination.

During and emergency it is important to have a contact that all of your household can call or email if they can not contact the head(s) of household during an emergency. Keep in mind it might be easier to make a long distance call than to call nearby during a disaster.

Ensure accurate and up-to-date contact details for all designated contacts.

Designated Contact Role Examples
  • Emergency Coordinator: Coordinates communication and actions during an emergency.
  • Evacuation Coordinator: Oversees and assists with the evacuation process.
  • Safety Checker: Ensures the safety and well-being of all members.
  • Communication Relay: Assists in relaying messages between members.
  • Pet Care Coordinator: Ensures the safety and well-being of pets.
  • Resource Manager: Coordinates essential resources distribution.
  • Reunification Coordinator: Helps arrange safe reunion points if separated.
  • Neighborhood Contact: Connects with neighbors for community support.
What is a 2-Way Radio?

A 2-way radio, also known as a walkie-talkie, is a portable communication device that allows two or more people to communicate wirelessly over short distances. It operates on specific radio frequencies, enabling real-time voice transmission between users.

2-way radios are widely used for various purposes, including outdoor activities, emergency preparedness, events coordination, and professional use in industries such as construction, security, and hospitality.

These radios are valuable in emergency situations, especially when cellular networks may be unreliable. They offer a quick and direct means of communication within a limited range, facilitating efficient team communication and coordination.
