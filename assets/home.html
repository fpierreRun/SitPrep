Where will you gather if your home is not accessible?

It is a good idea to choose an emergency meeting place for your household in case you are apart during an emergency. Be sure to select a place that is accessible and known to everyone, such as a landmark, intersections, or public park. If you have a pet, make sure the location is animal friendly.











What will be your evacuation Route?

Evacuation from your home can happen for many kind of emergencies. It is important that you have a plan to evacuate safely and quickly. Plan how will you leave and where you and your household will go if evacuation is in place.







Who will those in your household call or email during an emergency situation?

During and emergency it is important to have a contact that all of your household can call or email if they can not contact the head(s) of household during an emergency. Keep in mind it might be easier to make a long distance call than to call nearby during a disaster.
