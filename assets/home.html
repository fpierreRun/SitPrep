Step 1/3: Where will you gather if your home is not accessible?

Be sure to select a place that is accessible and known to everyone, such as a landmark, intersections, or public park. If you have a pet, make sure the location is animal friendly.











Step 2/3: What will be your evacuation Route?

Plan how will you leave and where you and your household will go if evacuation is in place.







Step 3/3: Who will those in your household call or email during an emergency situation?

Keep in mind it might be easier to make a long distance call than to call nearby during a disaster.
