Our Journey and Mission at SitPrep

Hello, I'm Dione, the founder of SitPrep. I consider myself not only your friend but also a difference-maker. Let me share the story behind SitPrep and why I'm passionate about helping you prepare for emergencies.

In 2020, the COVID-19 pandemic exposed the lack of preparedness in our society. Many people, including myself, were caught off guard. Witnessing the struggles of individuals trying to find essential supplies during that time, a few friends and I took action. We created local Facebook pages to coordinate efforts and provide support.

This experience inspired me as a UX designer to create a prototype for a tool that could help individuals like you create a 3-day plan and eventually a long-term plan for emergencies. I began developing it, learning to code along the way in 2022. However, I felt it wasn't ready until a recent personal experience highlighted its importance.

Picture this. I found myself stranded in a small town, miles away from home, all because of a flat tire. It happened on a Saturday night when all the tire shops were closed. Despite my best efforts, I couldn't find any local help, leaving me feeling helpless. The incident reminded me of the vulnerability we experienced during the chaotic days of the COVID-19 pandemic when supplies were scarce.

Fortunately, my incredible sister Kirsten and her husband Josh came to my rescue the next day, helping me get back on the road. Their support made all the difference.

Now, here's the revelation. I've been developing a website to help you prepare for emergencies, but I've always strived for perfection before launching it. However, those few days of being stranded made me realize the urgency of sharing the website with all of you. It's crucial for you to embrace the preparedness mindset rather than waiting for an emergency to strike before taking action.

At SitPrep, our mission is to empower you to be ready for any emergency. We believe that preparedness is not just a reaction but a proactive approach to safeguarding your well-being and that of your loved ones. Our website will provide you with the tools, resources, and guidance to create a comprehensive emergency plan, ensuring peace of mind and resilience in the face of unforeseen circumstances.

Don't wait for a moment of helplessness. Join us on this journey and let's prepare together.

Remember, SitPrep is here for you.

Warm regards,

Dione
