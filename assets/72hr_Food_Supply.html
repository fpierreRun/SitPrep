The Importance of at Least a 72-Hour Food Supply

Events happen all over the world that draw global aid, concern, and attention from masses of people. Whenever a disaster hits a community or country, the importance of requiring emergency kits increases. We all see that there are massive efforts when it comes to getting aid to the disaster-stricken location. However, the truth is that there are often a lot of logistical problems when it comes to this and so getting the help required straight away is not always possible. This highlights why it is critical for all individuals to be prepared by having an emergency food supply and kit in place. Continue reading to discover why this is so important and why it is something no business can afford to ignore.

Tragic events can include the likes of tsunamis, severe outbreaks of a serious illness, earthquakes, tornadoes, hurricanes, and such like. If any type of event like this happens in your area, you may not be able to get access to basic necessities until assistance and aid arrives. In a lot of cases, it can be several days before medical assistance, water, and food arrives. During these moments, you need to be self-reliant, and this is why you need to ensure that you have a 72-hour supply of food available.

Storing supplies for any emergency at your home is a critical part of keeping you and your loved ones safe should a situation arise. If there is a catastrophic incident in your location, you could find yourself without all of the simple products you need for daily life, from food to electricity. You may not even be able to access water. You could also find that your property has suffered structural damage and, therefore, it is not safe to occupy any more. This is why emergency preparedness is critical.

There are a number of different things that need to be considered when you put together your 72-hour supply kit. Yes, food and water are imperative, and this is always where you should begin when putting your kit together. However, you also need to consider the likes of emergency lights for safety, as well as emergency radios for communication. Another important factor to consider is the first aid. An emergency first aid kit should be comprehensive, including everything from gauze pads and rolls to bandages, antiseptics, and an assortment of compresses. You can consult with an expert to ensure that you have everything you need.

As you can see, the importance of at least a 72-hour food supply and kit cannot be ignored. If there is anything that 2020 has taught us, it is that you never truly know what is around the corner. It does not take a lot of effort to put together a 72-hour supply kit, yet this is something that could make a monumental difference if a disaster does happen in your area.
